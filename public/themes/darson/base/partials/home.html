Excellence Delivered, Every Time.

With global reach to over 25 countries, Darson Industries is one of the leading manufacturers of rubber and hoses for automotive & other industries. Our commitment to unparalleled quality and superior customer service has helped sustain our reputation for more than 60 years. Putting our customers’ needs first, we provide efficient and cost-effective solutions that deliver maximum value to businesses.
Using state-of-the-art technologies, Darson Industries integrates the whole production. A number of dedicated plants for processes such as compounding, extrusion, braiding, knitting, molding and testing ensure that the product matches the set standards. Quality being our prime focus, Darson Industries provides a complete solution tailored according to varied customer needs.
